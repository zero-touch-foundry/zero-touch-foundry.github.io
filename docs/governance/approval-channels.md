Stack Automation role: Account admin

While organizations typically want to enable their users to deploy solutions quickly and easily, in certain situations it may be necessary to put guardrails in place to ensure compliance with security policies or budget constraints. Defining an approval flow can provide an effective way to balance the need for agility and control.

Stack Automation's Approval channels define the users who can approve deployment launch requests. An approval channel is basically a list of users. Currently, Stack Automation supports 4 approval channels - Teams, Webex, Emails, and ServiceNow.

__To create an approval channel:__
1. Go to __Governance > Approval Channels__.
2. Click __Add Channel__.
3. Give the approval channel a __Name__ and optionally a __Description__.
4. Select the channel - Teams, Webex, Email or Service Now.
5. For Teams and Webex, enter the __Webhook address__.
6. For Service Now, please see [the following article](/eco-system/itsm-tools/service-now.md) for instructions.
7. Select the __Approvers__.
8. Click __Save__.
